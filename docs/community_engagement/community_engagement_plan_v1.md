This document outlines a basic engagement plan to build understanding and trust around the Festus CCDI project.

## 1. Objectives

- Explain the project in plain language.
- Gather and incorporate resident feedback.
- Provide ongoing transparency on decisions and impacts.

## 2. Key Audiences

- Nearby residents and neighborhoods.
- City leadership and staff.
- Schools and local institutions.
- Business community and civic groups.

## 3. Engagement Channels

- Public meetings and town halls.
- Small-group briefings with key stakeholders.
- Project website and email updates.
- Printed materials for residents without reliable internet access.

## 4. Core Messages

- Community control of land and covenants via FDIA/FCIC.
- Clear protections around noise, water, and environment.
- Expected economic benefits and how revenues are used.
- Opportunities for local input and investment (including future bonds).

## 5. Feedback and Accountability

- Structured ways to submit questions and concerns.
- Commitments on response times.
- Regular public reporting on project metrics and community benefits.
