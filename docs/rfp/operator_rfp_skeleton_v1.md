This document provides a high-level skeleton for a Request for Proposals (RFP) to select a private operator for the Festus Community-Controlled Digital Infrastructure project.

## 1. Introduction and Background

- Description of Festus CCDI and objectives.
- Role of FDIA/FCIC as platform owner (land + covenants + ground lease).
- Summary of community priorities (jobs, tax base, noise/water protections, transparency).

## 2. Project Scope

- Site description and zoning context.
- Expected power capacity, facility scale, and phasing.
- Required on-site and off-site infrastructure (roads, utilities, water, etc.).

## 3. Governance and Community Controls

- Explanation of FDIA/FCIC governance and oversight.
- Summary of key covenants the operator must accept:
  - Noise limits and monitoring.
  - Water usage caps and reporting.
  - Environmental and traffic management.
  - Expansion thresholds and approval processes.

## 4. Desired Operator Qualifications

- Experience with similar facilities or infrastructure.
- Financial strength and access to capital.
- Track record on environmental, social, and community engagement metrics.
- Relevant technical and operational capabilities.

## 5. Commercial Structure

- Overview of proposed ground lease terms.
- Expected revenue-sharing and fee structure (e.g., per-MW fees, revenue-based fees).
- High-level expectations for capital stack (debt/equity) and non-recourse nature to FDIA/FCIC.

## 6. Proposal Requirements

- Technical proposal content (design, operations, timeline).
- Financial proposal content (capex, opex, funding plan, returns).
- Community benefits plan (local hiring, education, partnerships).
- ESG commitments and reporting.

## 7. Evaluation Criteria

- Weighted scoring across:
  - Technical quality and reliability.
  - Financial robustness and bankability.
  - Community and environmental benefits.
  - Alignment with FDIA/FCIC governance and covenants.

## 8. Process and Timeline

- RFP issue date, Q&A window, and site visit schedule.
- Submission deadlines.
- Evaluation phases (shortlisting, interviews, best-and-final offers).
- Expected date for selection and financial close.

## 9. Legal and Compliance

- Applicable local and state laws and regulations.
- Confidentiality and public records considerations.
- Instructions on how to submit questions and receive official responses.

## 10. Attachments (to be developed)

- Draft ground lease term sheet.
- Summary of covenants and performance metrics.
- Data room index (site studies, baseline data, etc.).
